This Isn't Background Music

Two professional songwriters with major publishing deals sit down in your living room. For 90 minutes, they perform six original songs and share the stories that inspired them. You've probably heard their tracks on the radio without knowing who wrote them.

You can ask anything. How'd that song end up on country radio? What was the original version? Guests consistently tell us it's more memorable than the Grand Ole Opry or the Bluebird Cafe.
