Writers Round
Nashville

Your Unique Venue Atmosphere

When you step into our exclusive songwriter sessions, you're not just attending a performance. You're experiencing the heart and soul of Nashville's music scene in the most intimate and authentic way possible. Picture a private setting, reminiscent of iconic spots like the Bluebird Cafe, but with a personal twist that makes each session truly unique.

At Casa Concert, we bring the charm and authenticity of Nashville's legendary songwriters' rounds right into your living room. A writers' round is a one-of-a-kind live music experience where talented songwriters perform their original works and share the deeply personal stories behind the songs.
