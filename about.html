About Us

Bringing Nashville's Songwriting Magic Home
With Casa Concert

It Started With a Song

Rob Ridgeway created Spontuneous, a music-driven board game that became the #1 selling game on Amazon. But the game only works because of one thing: songs people love. And songs people love only exist because of songwriters most people never hear about.

That realization stuck with him. "Without songwriters, Spontuneous wouldn't exist, Casa Concert wouldn't exist, and your favorite songs wouldn't exist." So he spent two years building something that gives those songwriters a stage and gives you an experience you can't get anywhere else.

Better Than the Bluebird

Guests consistently tell us their Casa Concert experience was more memorable than the Grand Ole Opry or the Bluebird Cafe. That's the difference between sitting in a crowd of strangers and having two hit songwriters performing three feet away from you in your own living room.

Every session runs about 90 minutes. Two professional songwriters with major publishing deals perform six original compositions. Some of those songs ended up on country radio. Some haven't been released yet. You'll hear the stories behind them, ask anything you want, and walk away understanding music in a completely different way.
