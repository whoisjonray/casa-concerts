FAQ

Questions? We Have Answers.

Our songwriter sessions offer an intimate and interactive experience that larger venues can't provide. You're not in a crowd of strangers. You're in your own living room with two professional songwriters performing three feet away. You hear the stories behind the songs, ask whatever you want, and get access to unreleased material. Guests regularly tell us it beats the Bluebird Cafe and the Grand Ole Opry.

Each session lasts about 90 minutes. Two professional songwriters perform six original songs while sharing industry stories, the creative process behind their work, and insider insights about the music business.

Our sessions focus on original compositions, but they're highly interactive. The songwriters engage with the audience throughout, and you might even get to hear songs that haven't been released yet. It's a conversation, not a concert.

All of our songwriters have major publishing deals and have written songs for some of today's biggest artists. Some of the songs they perform may become chart-topping hits. You might be hearing tomorrow's #1 single before anyone else.
