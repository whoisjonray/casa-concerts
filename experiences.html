What To Expect

Your Unique Venue Atmosphere

Picture this: you're sitting in your living room, and two songwriters who've had songs on country radio are performing right in front of you. It's like having the Bluebird Cafe in your house, except there's no wait list, no crowd, and no one between you and the music.

The atmosphere is personal and authentic. It's just you, your guests, and the songwriters. Every performance is different because every conversation is different. That's what makes it special.

Discover Tomorrow's Hits

90 Minutes You Won't Forget

Over 90 minutes, two professional songwriters with major publishing deals perform six original songs, giving you a front-row seat to what could be tomorrow's chart-topping hits.

They'll share the stories behind each song, answer your questions, and maybe even play something nobody else has heard yet. It's a window into the creative process of people whose songs you've been singing along to without ever knowing their names.

Just a Few Clicks Away

The intimacy of Casa Concert can't be replicated at larger venues. We bring the full experience to your living room, your Airbnb, your vacation rental, or your event space. Nashville comes to you.
